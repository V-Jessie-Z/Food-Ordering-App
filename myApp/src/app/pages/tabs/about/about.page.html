About About Welcome to the Ordering App! Here's a quick overview of its features and functionality: Home Page
  • Slider and Restaurant Listings: The home page greets you with a dynamic slider and a comprehensive list of restaurants.
  • Restaurant Details: Click on any restaurant to view its details.
  • Ordering Made Easy: Simply click on a restaurant to automatically add it to your cart as an order.
 Search Page
  • Search Functionality: The search page allows you to easily find restaurants.
  • Seamless Ordering: Just like on the home page, clicking a restaurant in the search results adds it to your cart as an order.
 Cart
  • Order Summary: The cart displays the price of each item ordered and the total price, including VAT.
  • Payment Confirmation: Click the "Make Payment" button to bring up a modal that confirms your payment.
 Account Page
  • User Details: View and edit your personal details.
  • Order History: Review past orders and reorder with a single click, adding the previous order to your cart as a new order.
  • Manage Addresses: This option is for decorative purposes only and does not have functional capabilities.
